TUKLAS

A Submission to the BARMM Data Challenge

Description

We created an interactive dashboard to track, monitor, and evaluate metrics of development in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

Project Abstract

The "transition period" is a crucial phase towards the success and full-adaptation of BARMM (the Annex of Normalization). The premise of Project Tuklas are the importance that the transition period was intended for: a) setup and prove tangible improvements over previous systems, and b) demonstrate promising capacity to deliver the objectives defined in the Framework Agreement on the Bangsamoro.
Project Tuklas will be a tool for investigating and monitoring the state of BARMM (2018-reference, when BOL was signed into effectivity) in these two regard through: a) a comparative analysis of BARMM before and after 2018, and b) progress monitoring towards objectives based on development plans.
The Annex of Normalization determined three key areas crucial for evaluating the success of the transition period (to measure performance of implemented frameworks): a) security, b) socio-economic development, and c) transitional justice.
Each of these areas are assessed as component to BARMM's overall development assessment through the "comparative analysis + progress monitoring" methodology of Project Tuklas. For each area, indicators were determined from the Bangsamoro Roadmaps, 1st Bangsamoro Development Plan (BDP), Annex of Normalization, and the Framework Agreement on the Bangsamoro.
